RANCE HUFF, Chief of Staff

Rance has served as Council Member Williams’ Chief of Staff for two years and in the NYC Council for 12 years. He previously served as Chief of Staff for former NYC Council Member Leroy Comrie (Queens) and as Communications Director for former NYC Council Member Yvette D. Clarke (Brooklyn). His responsibilities include operations oversight of the Legislative and District Offices. He proudly resides in the 45th District with his wife and children.

rhuff@council.nyc.gov  

NICK E. SMITH, J.D., Legislative & Communications Director

Nick serves as Council Member Williams’ Director of Legislation and Communications. He previously served in the same role for NYS Assembly Member Linda B. Rosenthal, in addition to serving as an appointed Commissioner for the City of Berkeley, CA (2004-2007) and as coordinator of the national non-partisan collaborative, Education from the Inside Out Coalition. His responsibility includes managing the Council Member’s legislative portfolio and media strategy.

nsmith@council.nyc.gov

K.BAIN, Budget Director

K. Bain has been the Budget Director for the 45th Council manic district in Brooklyn since 2010. His role includes duties such as; participating in the balancing New York City’s 70 billion dollar annual budget, as well as direct oversight of a multi-million dollar member item budget. In his previous role as Legislative Director, K was instrumental in the drafting, development and enactment of numerous of pieces of legislation, most recently the Community Safety Act.

kbain@council.nyc.gov
